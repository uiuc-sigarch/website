Welcome to SIGARCH

University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign's student-run computer architecture interest group

Meetings

We host weekly meetings Wednesdays from 5:00-6:00pm in ECEB 3015. These meetings use a split format, with the first half devoted to the high-level summary of a relevant topic, and the second half devoted to the discussion of a paper. For an archive of past meetings, see here.

Workshops

Since Fall 2023, we have been hosting semi-regular weekend workshops directed at giving members hands-on exposure to projects in computer architecture and chip design. For an archive of past workshops, see here.
